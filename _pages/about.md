About me
======
Hi, I'm Caleb.  I am currently a PhD Candidate in Computer Science (artificial intelligence & logic) at Indiana University, with the co-supervision of [Saúl Blanco](http://homes.sice.indiana.edu/sblancor/) and [Larry Moss](https://iulg.sitehost.iu.edu/moss/).

My research is in neuro-symbolic AI.  I do both foundational work (on the formal relationship between neural networks and logic) as well as more immediately applied work (developing hybrid neuro-symbolic systems).

My work cuts across field lines, and so I end up spreading myself across many different groups and seminars.  I'm proudly involved in:

[IU Logic Seminar](https://iulg.sitehost.iu.edu/) ○ [Cognitive Lunch](https://cogs.sitehost.iu.edu/events/cognitive-lunch.php) ○ [Deep Learning & CBR Group](http://vision.soic.indiana.edu/projects/deepcbr/) ○ [plwonks](http://wonks.github.io/)

<center> ○ </center>  

At home, I live with my kaleidoscopic partner Sabrina and our two lovely cats [Gunter](https://ais-climber.github.io/images/gunter2.jpg) and [Garnet](https://ais-climber.github.io/images/garnet.jpg).  When I can tear myself away from work :-) we like to read webcomics and take our cats out for walks.
