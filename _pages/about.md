Welcome!  I'm a PhD Candidate in Computer Science at Indiana University, co-supervised by [Larry Moss](https://iulg.sitehost.iu.edu/moss/) and [Saúl Blanco](https://homes.luddy.indiana.edu/sblancor).  I'm a member of the [IU logic group](https://iulg.sitehost.iu.edu/) and [plwonks](https://wonks.github.io/).

## Research
I study the foundations of machine learning and cognition, often through the lens of formal logic. My current work is in the theory of neuro-symbolic AI, through which I hope to (1) better understand the power and limitations of neural network learning, (2) bridge the gap between neural network learning and belief revision, and (3) explore the possibility of neural network alignment.
Besides this, I have a soft spot for dynamic epistemic logics, natural language inference, and 

philosophical issues in AI and cognitive science.
