This page is a list of my friends, family, and acquaintances throughout the years, inspired by [this page](https://mason.gmu.edu/~rhanson/friends.html).  Mostly this is to help me remember, reminisce, and keep up with everybody, but also is convenient for introducing friends to each other.  This list doesn't distinguish between close friends, friends, and acquaintances -- in part to avoid offending people, but also because these lines in the sand get washed over as the tides change.

This list is very much a work in progress.  If you see your name here and we haven't spoken in a while, please take this as an invitation to say hi and catch up!  If you don't see your name here, that's not a slight against you. I'd appreciate it if you let me know though, since I am trying to remember everyone who is important in my life.

Also, if you have a cute or shiny webpage you'd like advertised here, just let me know; I'm very happy to signal boost a friend!  If you want your name or webpage removed, I will do that as well.
