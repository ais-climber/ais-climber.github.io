Webcomics I like

Comics have been a big part of my life since I was little. I started out on American superhero comics and Japanese manga, and eventually got hooked on webcomics. Here are some of my favorites—I hope you enjoy reading these as much as I do!

  • xkcd and smbc (the classic science nerd comics)

  • Dumbing of Age (takes place at Indiana University!)

  • Questionable Content (peaceful slice-of-life to drink morning tea to)

  • Nine Planets Without Intelligent Life (philosophy disguised as a webcomic about robots)

  • Rock and Riot (super cute exploration of gender expectations)

  • The Property of Hate (hard to summarize, but it's a masterpiece)

  • Missing Monday (a very sweet romance with an Alice in Wonderland vibe)

  • Alice and the Nightmare (the comparison with Alice in Wonderland is more on the nose...)

  • My Obsession with Chess (a memoir about obsession and escapism)

  • Broodhollow – (Twin Peaks-esque supernatural horror, on hiatus :( )
